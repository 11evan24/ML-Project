# Contribution Guidelines

Thank you for considering contributing to this project! We welcome your suggestions and improvements.

Please note that this project is released with a [Contributor Code of Conduct](code-of-conduct.md). By participating in this project, you agree to abide by its terms.

---

### Pull Request Guidelines

To help us maintain the quality and focus of the project, please ensure your pull requests adhere to the following guidelines:

- **High-Quality Content**: Ensure that your contributions are thoughtful, well-researched, and valuable to the community.
- **Clear Relevance**: Clearly describe how your content or changes are relevant to the project's goals (specifically to RR).
- **Focus on Advanced Tools**: Please refrain from adding universally known or fundamental tools (e.g., Git, GitHub, R, RStudio, Jupyter) to the primary resource lists. Our goal is to highlight specialized resources, so please direct beginners to standard educational materials instead.

Thank you for your contributions!
